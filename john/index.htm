Welcome To VIS CareerConnections Co-op
 

Mission Statement

To help people in career transition by creating a cooperative environment whereby members can capitalize on the synergy of talented people by practicing skills in a mentor/intern fashion.  Members aspire to offer individual and project-specific consulting at low cost to clients who are interested in supporting this community.  Where feasible the Co-op program provides a learning environment as well as the collaborative infrastructure for facilitating our work.
